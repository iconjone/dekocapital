Entrepreneurs investing in entrepreneurs

Founded by entrepreneurs, we understand the challenges that go into building successful businesses. Our entrepreneurial roots provide us with a unique perspective when forming partnerships with founders and family operators.

Good Business Stewards

We will honor the traditions and legacy of your business and it is important to us that all stakeholders have aligned interests and working towards the same goals.

Straight Shooters

We strongly believe mutual trust and respect are essential in achieving outsized success for all stakeholders.

Comfortable with Complexity

Every business situation is unique and Dekos is comfortable dealing with complex situations such as corporate carve-out, family owned businesses, restructurings, or backing management led corporate restarts.
